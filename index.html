How It Works

Your Child's Journey in 8 Weeks

From first enrolment to certificate ceremony — here's exactly what to expect.

01

Enrol & Choose Your Band

Select the right age band (4–7, 8–10, or 11–13). Pay one fixed price — done. No recurring fees, no per-session charges.

02

Weeks 1–3: Immersion

Your child engages with the curriculum through interactive, age-appropriate activities — phonics games, creative writing, rhetorical analysis. Built on proven British English pedagogy.

03

Week 4: First Milestone Check-in

We pause for a structured review. A fun, low-pressure check-in activity reveals progress. Parents receive a clear progress report on what's mastered and what needs more practice.

04

Weeks 5–7: Deepening

Building on the first half, children tackle more advanced material — digraphs and sentences for younger ones, persuasive writing for mid-level, and formal registers for older students.

05

Week 8: Final Milestone & Certificate

Final assessment through an engaging capstone activity. Every child who completes the programme receives a printed-style progress certificate by name — a tangible record of their achievement.
